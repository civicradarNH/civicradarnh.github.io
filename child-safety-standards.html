Child Safety Standards

CivicRadar · Effective 3 July 2026 · Operator: CivicRadar

Not an official municipal app. Independent community hazard map for Mumbai, Pune, and Thane.

CivicRadar is committed to preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), including child sexual abuse material (CSAM). This page describes our standards, moderation practices, and how to report concerns.

1. Intended audience

CivicRadar is for users 18+ only. We do not target children. See our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

2. Prohibited content

  • CSAM or sexual content involving minors
  • Content that grooms or exploits children
  • Hazard photos focused on identifying minors

Reports must show the hazard site, not people.

3. Prevention and moderation

  • On-device upload checks and EXIF stripping
  • Public map limited to hazard pins — no private user messaging
  • Operators can remove violating reports
  • In-app deletion: Profile → Delete my data

4. Report concerns

Email nilesh.hadawale@gmail.com with subject CivicRadar — Child safety report. Do not forward illegal material. We remove content and report to authorities as required by law.
